OUR STORY

OUR HISTORY

  • Like many students pursuing chartered accountancy, we, the founders of Lazy Bone faced a lot of difficulty juggling between our chartered accountancy studies, coaching classes, internship and college exams. That clicked something in our mind.
  • Lazy Bone Publications was, therefore, founded in May 2012 with the intention of making short & crisp books for college students studying in degree college and pursuing professional courses alongwith it. We first published books on two subjects of F.Y.B.Com.
  • In the later part of the year, we published books for two subjects of T.Y.B.Com. These books did reasonably well. The concept, style and content were appreciated by many. That really set the ball rolling for us!!
  • At present, we provide our notes to more than 350 institutes and touch lives of more than 25,000 students. By the end of this year (2015-16), we will be selling around 1 lakh books. We happily employ a team of around 15 people. Apart from this, many other jobworkers, authors, handwriters etc. work with us on a freelance basis.
  • An important part of our story is our innovation arm – Lazy Bone Support Services (LBSS). This is an initiative that is etched deep in our storyline. At LBSS, we develop audio visual presentations and mobile apps which have helped students to enhance their learning experience by leaps & bounds.
